What changed from v1

  • Dense header strip — all patient identity, risk, plan, allergies, active Rx in two compact rows. Pinned, always visible.
  • Left sidebar sub-nav — replaces horizontal tabs. Care Management section expands to show Timeline, Care Protocols, Assessments, Tasks, Goals, etc. Much more navigable depth.
  • AI summary is contextual — sits at the top of Care Protocols as "Care Protocol so far", not isolated in its own tab. Care managers see the AI briefing in context of the protocol steps below it.
  • Protocol steps as accordion cards — completed/in-progress/pending/overdue states. Each expandable to show individual tasks within.
  • Right metadata rail — assignee, enrollment date, tier change history, next review date, CRS score. Persistent context.
  • Action chips on AI summary — Schedule PCP, Send Outreach, Order Lab. One-click actions generated by AI.
